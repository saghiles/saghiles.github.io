About

I am a third year Phd student at Paris Descartes University, working on machine learning under the supervision of professor Mohamed Nadif. I curruntely develop scalable and effective model-based (co-)clustering approaches for high dimensional sparse data. With a particular focus on the tasks of text document clustering and item recommendation.

Research Interests

My main research interest include machine learning, data mining and their application to text mining, recommender systems and bioinformatics. In particular I focus on developing scalable and effetive unsupervised learning approaches, tailored for high dimensional sparse data, for the tasks of text documents clustering, item recommendation, dimentionality reduction and visualization.

A brief History

Aghiles Salah is graduated from Paris Descartes University where he received his Master degree in Machine Learning in 2013. Thereafter, he obtained a grant from doctoral school EDITE DE PARIS in October 2013. He is currently a Ph.D. student at Paris Descartes University, and a member of the Machine Learning for Data science (MLDS) group.
