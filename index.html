I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Singapore Management University (SMU) and memeber of the Preferred.AI group. My research falls into statistical learning, I am particularly interested in unsupervised learning, generative models, approximate inference/learning, and their application to problems such as personalized recommendation, topic modeling, representation learning, natural language processing and other related topics.

I am also leading development of Cornac, which is a Python Framework for Multimodal Recommender Systems.
